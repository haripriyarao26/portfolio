# Certification Images

Place your LinkedIn certification screenshots here.

## File Naming Convention:
- `agentic-ai-developers.png` or `.jpg`
- `hands-on-agentic-ai-llamaindex.png` or `.jpg`
- `generative-ai-llm.png` or `.jpg`
- `what-is-generative-ai.png` or `.jpg`

## How to Add Images:

1. Save your LinkedIn certification screenshots in this folder
2. Update the `imageUrl` in `data/resume.ts` to point to the image:
   - Example: `imageUrl: "/certifications/agentic-ai-developers.png"`
